# 📊 **UPFLYOVER DASHBOARD MODULES SPECIFICATION**

## **🔹 COMPANY DASHBOARD (Client View)**

### **Core Modules**
1. **Trust Badge + Verification Status**
   - KYC verification badge (Verified/Pending/Rejected)
   - Trust score display (0-100)
   - Verification level indicators
   - Compliance status badges

2. **Feed (Social Business Network)**
   - Post project requirements
   - Share company updates
   - Industry news and insights
   - Success stories and case studies
   - Like, comment, share functionality

3. **Inbox (Communication Hub)**
   - Direct enquiries from potential partners
   - Negotiation threads
   - Contract discussions
   - System notifications
   - Real-time messaging

4. **Deals Management**
   - **EOI (Expression of Interest)** - Submit/receive interest
   - **RFQ (Request for Quotation)** - Create/respond to quotes
   - **Quotations** - Price proposals and negotiations
   - **Deal History** - Past transactions and outcomes
   - **Pipeline Tracking** - Deal stages and progress

5. **Trust Log (Reputation System)**
   - Past transaction history
   - Client/supplier ratings (1-5 stars)
   - Testimonials and reviews
   - Performance metrics
   - Dispute resolution history

### **Additional Client Modules**
6. **Analytics Dashboard**
   - Profile views and engagement
   - Lead generation metrics
   - Response rates and conversion
   - Market insights and trends

7. **Network Management**
   - Saved companies and contacts
   - Partnership requests
   - Referral tracking
   - Business connections map

8. **Document Center**
   - Contract templates
   - Signed agreements
   - Compliance certificates
   - Export/import documentation
